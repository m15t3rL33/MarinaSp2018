Biggest Fears

There are a big variety of phobias in the world. Phobias are fears that people are extremely scared of. Some of the most common phobias are acrophobia and arachnophobia. Acrophobia is the fear of heights and being on high-elevated places. The fear of spiders is known as arachnophobia. If these phobias become too extreme, terrible things could happen, such as panic attacks.

One of my biggest fears are spiders and insects. The scientific name for a fear of spiders is arachnophobia. A fear of insects is known as entomophobia. If I see a bug at my house, I would always ask my parents to come and kill it. I've gotten better at staying calm when I see a bug, but I still wouldn't want it to stay there. The worst part is when they never move when you're awake, but they're gone when you wake up.
