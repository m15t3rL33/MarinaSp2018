It's like a free version of Photoshop, but with less tools, but it's still good since it's free. It only needs the user to remember all the buttons and what they do.

However, I still hope that 9th grade wouldn't be too hard, but wouldn't be as easy as 8th grade. Well, after I complete high school, I would go to college. That's what my parents want to go, but I don't know if I should. Completely finishing high school should give me enough knowledge on all subjects. Going to college would just make me an expert on some subjects, but would take up 4 years of my life. It would also cost a lot of money, but my parents don't mind it.
