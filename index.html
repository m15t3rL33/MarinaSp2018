Engineering 101: building on hope

by APB on 22 March 2018

A ragtag bunch of students from all walks of life; chosen to take the first steps into broadening their perspectives with a very motivated teacher new to Marina Middle School. This collection of pages is the students first attempt at using HTML cobbling with text editor to carve out their little niche in the web. Through this, they hope to answer questions, begin to figure out how to solve problems, and to create questions on their own.

The sample you see linked to this page stem from the students' humble beginning pages greeting, "Hello World." to their first assigned page.
