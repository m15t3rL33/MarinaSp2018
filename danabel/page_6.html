Lowell 9th, 10th, 11th, and 12th Grade Requirements
Students who apply to Lowell must live in San Francisco and must stay a resident at San Francisco while attending Lowell. All applications must be submitted by December 15,2017. Lowell students who have not taken SBAC test, must take the Lowell submission test. You must do an essay on one of the harshisps you had and how you overcame it.
Lowell starts at 7:30 everyday, you can choose to have 6 or 7 classes. 6 classes means you have 2 hours of lunch/free time and 7 classes means you have 1 hour of lunch/free time.
