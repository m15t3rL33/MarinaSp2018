My Favorite Music Artist- SZA

Her Genre- R&B/Soul

SZA's full name is Solana Imani Rowe. She released her album 'CTRL', which launched on June 9, 2017. 'CTRL' also hit Number 3 on the Billboard Top 200. Some songs in her album include 'Love Galore', 'The Weekend', and 'Broken Clocks'. She was also feautured on a couple songs, including 'What Lovers Do' by Maroon 5, 'All the Stars' with Kendrick Lamar, and 'I Do' with Cardi B.

Some of my favorites from her would have to be 'Love Galore' and 'The Weekend', along with her new song with Cardi B, 'I Do'. After listening to these songs, I started listening to more R&B music. Some R&B artists I started listening to more were Drake and The Weeknd. Most of their songs belong into the Hip-Hop and Rap category, but I listen to their R&B songs as well. I have no idea why it is my favorite genre of music, but I guess it's just because I like how the background tune is very calming. I've always liked Hip-Hop and Rap, and the R&B music I listen to are very similar.

SZA was nominated for 5 Grammy awards, btu unfortunately won none of them. She had a nomination for Best New Artist. Her song 'The Weekend' had a nomination for Best R&B Performance. 'Supermodel' had a nominee for Best R&B Song. Her debut album 'CTRL' had a nominee for Best Urban Contemporary Album. Her song 'Love Galore' with Travis Scott had a nominee for Best Rap/Sung Performance.
