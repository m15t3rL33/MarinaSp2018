Mean Girls- Lindsey Lohan

Lindsey Lohan was born on July 2, 1986. She was a model at just 3 years old, making appearances in television commercials. She started acting when she was 10, playing Ali Fowler in the television drama 'Another World'. The peak of her success started with her starring on Disney's 'The Parent Trap'. This lead her to star in more movies including 'Just My Luck', and 'Herbie- Fully Loaded'.

After her break from acting in 2005, she was casted into the comedic film 'Freaky Friday'. The movie became a big hit, and she won a MTV Movie Award for Best Breakthrough Female. Her next project was acting in the movie 'Mean Girls'. She played the role of 'Caty Heron'. Fun fact, but she originally wanted the role of Regina George, but bshe was afraid that being a 'mean girl' would ruin her reputation.

Some accomplishments of Lohan was her signing into Casablanca Records, and her album 'Speak' was released in 2004, while her album 'A Little More Personal' was launched in 2005. She also released her own fashion line, selling her production of women's leggings. At the end of the year, she became an artistic designer for Ungaro, a fashion house. She won a Kids Choice Award for 'Best Female Actress' in 2006.
